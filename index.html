Welcome

This is my data recording website for 'the red book'! This site logs my favorite posts on 'the red book' over a week.

Simply hit 'start,' and you'll encounter seven mountains, each symbolizing a day of the week on the homepage. Click on a mountain to access the corresponding day's content, and click on the green dot on that page to access detailed data.

Don't worry if you forget the steps; you can always find the instruction tucked away in the top right corner of the homepage for quick reference.

Enjoy exploring my website!
